KARDDER
Kardder is a social networking app utilizing location-based networking to help users find people and places nearby in real time. I worked with a team of UI/UX interns to discuss, develop, and implement app improvements and new features, focusing the app on the "nearby" and "now" of social networking.
ROLE: UI/UX Intern
DURATION: June 2022 - Present
SKILLS: Figma, User Research
NEARBY
The Holy Grail Feature: View active users within 0.3 miles from your location, in real time.
Impact:
- Enlarging the Nearby button and relocating it to the middle of the bottom bar.
- Introducing active status: A green dot shows that a user has been active on the app in the past hour.
CURRENTLY
A timeline to view posts of your following and all users within 10 miles from your location, posted within the past 24 hours.
Impact:
- Brainstorming post categories: a list of post content types that users can tag in their posts.
MOODS
We introduced moods as an additional embellishment to the profile to facilitate and spark interactions between users.
Impact:
- Brainstorming Mood categories and associated emojis.
PROFILE
We took steps to make profiles more personal, adding to existing details while creating new features for user to show what they're all about.
Impact:
- Collaborating on major profile changes such as the addition of moods, active status indicator, and past posts onto the profile.
